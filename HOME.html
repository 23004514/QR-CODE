Our Mission

Turning agriculture into South Africa's biggest youth opportunity

AgroYouth exists to reduce youth unemployment by connecting young people with careers in agriculture through education, technology, entrepreneurship, and direct employment opportunities.

We work alongside government, universities, agribusinesses, and mentors to build clear pathways — from a first internship to a thriving, technology-driven farming business.

The Problem

Why young people struggle to find agricultural jobs

Real barriers, not lack of interest — and each one is something AgroYouth is built to solve.

🧰

Limited practical experience

Few structured, hands-on placements are available to first-time job seekers.

🗺️

Limited access to land

Owning or leasing productive land is out of reach for most young people.

💰

Limited funding

Start-up capital and working capital remain the biggest blockers to entry.

📚

Lack of training

Formal agricultural education doesn't reach everyone who wants it.

👁️

Limited awareness

Many don't know modern agriculture even offers these careers.

🤝

Poor networking

Without industry contacts, opportunities are hard to find.

💻

Technology gaps

Digital literacy and access to tools lag behind industry needs.

🏙️

Urban migration

Young people leave rural areas before discovering agri-careers.
